This is all about animals

Kangaroos are the world's largest marsupials. A Red Kangaroo can weigh 90kg and can grow two metres tall. Black Wallaroos, at around 20kg, are the smallest species (their name a portmanteau of wallaby and kangaroo). Kangaroos are famous for hopping and can reach speeds of 60kph, clearing more than 8m in a single bound!-Bush Heritage Australia
